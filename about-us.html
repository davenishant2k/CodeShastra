Our Mission

We at HelpingHands aim to aid the society by our little contribution to help those in need. We connect people to NGOs so that they can find a place where they can belong and call home. We provide solution through our easy to use application wherein people can help the ones in need to find the right place for them.

We also aim to help the NGOs by providing a platform for our users to look at various NGOs, know what they stand for and give them a platform to donate and contribute whatever they can for the particular organisation We aim to encourage people in our society to contribute whatever little they can for the betterment of the environment they live in. We stand for a social cause to help the world move forward bye taking in account all the people / together with all into a better future.
